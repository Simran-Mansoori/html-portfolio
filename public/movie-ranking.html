The Best Movies According to Simran

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Jab we Met

This movie shows the story of a lively girl and a heartbroken businessman
meeting by chance on a train, leading to unexpected journey of love and self-discovery.

3 idiots

This movie tells us how one should live their life without chasing excellence.

Dhamaal

Four friends race to find hidden treasure, leading to hilarious chaos.
